  • password_hashstring
  • ageint
  • genderenum
  • edu_levelenum
  • english_levelenum
  • levelintFK
  • total_expint
  • weekly_expint
  • monthly_expint
  • streak_daysint
  • avatar_urlstring

  • created_attimestamp

5 fields
Akkaunt darajalari va XP chegaralari
